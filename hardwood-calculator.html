Installation Tips

  • Acclimation: Let wood acclimate 3-7 days before installation
  • Moisture Testing: Check subfloor and wood moisture levels
  • Stagger Joints: Maintain 6" minimum stagger for strength
  • Expansion Gaps: Leave ¬º" gap around perimeter
  • Direction: Install parallel to longest wall for best appearance

Hardwood Flooring FAQ

How do I calculate board feet for irregular room shapes?

Break the room into rectangles, calculate each area separately, then add them together. Add an extra 5% waste factor for complex shapes.

What's the difference between solid and engineered hardwood?

Solid hardwood is 100% wood, typically ¬æ" thick. Engineered has a wood veneer over plywood core, available in various thicknesses.

How much extra hardwood should I order?

Order 10-15% extra for straight installations, 15-25% for complex patterns. Keep extras for future repairs.

Can I install hardwood over concrete?

Engineered hardwood can be installed over concrete with proper moisture barrier. Solid hardwood typically requires a wood subfloor.
